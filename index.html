About the boxer

The ideal boxer is a medium sized dog, with short and strong limbs, and a tight-fitting coat. Develpoed to be a guard, working, and companion dog. The Boxer Combines strength and agility, with elegance and style. The chisled head of a boxer makes them out be a unique looking dog. The Boxer can also be called a "hearing" dog, because the Boxer is alert and watchful. Boxers need to be exposed to socialization at an early age of their life. Socialization helps you ensure that your Boxer grows up and is friendly and outgoing.
